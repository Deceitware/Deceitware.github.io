Hacking

Hacking is the practice of gaining unauthorized access to computer systems or networks.

DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service)

A DDoS attack involves overwhelming a system with traffic from multiple sources.

Phishing

Phishing involves tricking individuals into revealing sensitive information.

Ransomware

Ransomware involves encrypting data and demanding payment for its release.

Dark Web

The Dark Web refers to hidden networks and websites accessible via Tor or similar software.

Hping Attack

An Hping attack involves using the hping tool to send custom ICMP packets.

Brute Force

A Brute Force attack involves attempting to guess passwords or encryption keys.
